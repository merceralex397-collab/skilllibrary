# Danger Signals

These phrases often compress unstated assumptions:

- "we can just"
- "should be straightforward"
- "no major changes needed"
- "reuse the existing flow"
- "the provider already supports this"
- "ops can handle it"
- "we'll sort that out later"

Treat them as invitations to ask:

- what specifically makes this straightforward
- what dependency is being assumed
- who owns the unresolved part
- what evidence already exists
