# Evidence Rubric

Use this rubric while reviewing Skeptic Pass.

- Confirm the scope and claimed success condition first.
- Collect direct evidence for evidence collection, risk ordering, and assumption tracking.
- Separate observed facts from interpretation.
- Rank issues by user impact and rework cost.
